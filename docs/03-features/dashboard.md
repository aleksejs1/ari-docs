# Customizable Dashboard

The Ari dashboard is your personal command center. It displays key information about your contacts, upcoming events, and recent activity through a set of **widgets** that you can fully customize to match your workflow.

## Built-in Widgets

| Widget | Description |
|---|---|
| **System Stats** | Total contacts, audit logs, and sent notifications at a glance |
| **Recent Logins** | Your recent login activity with timestamps and details |
| **Upcoming Anniversaries** | Birthdays and important dates coming up in the next 30 days |
| **Catch Up** | Up to 7 contacts whose keep-in-touch cadence has elapsed, with a one-click Log Interaction button |
| **Groups** | Quick overview of your contact groups |
| **Recent Activity** | Latest changes to your contacts and system events |

## Toggling Widgets

Not every widget is relevant to every user. To show or hide widgets:

1. Click the **Customize** button (gear icon) in the top-right corner of the dashboard.
2. A panel appears listing all available widgets with checkboxes.
3. Toggle the widgets you want to see, and close the panel.

Your selection is saved automatically.

## Reordering Widgets

To rearrange widgets on your dashboard:

1. Click the **Reorder** button in the top-right corner. The dashboard enters **edit mode**.
2. Each widget shows a **drag handle** (grip icon) on its left edge.
3. Grab a widget by its handle and **drag it** up or down to reorder it within its current zone.
4. Click **Done** to save your changes, or **Cancel** to discard them.

## Moving Widgets Between Zones

In edit mode, you can also move widgets between layout zones:

1. While in edit mode, zone labels and borders become visible (e.g., "Full Width", "Left Column", "Right Column").
2. **Drag a widget** from one zone and **drop it** into a different zone.
3. The widget will appear in the target zone at the position where you dropped it.
4. Click **Done** to save.

## Changing the Layout

Ari offers several layout presets to organize your dashboard:

| Layout | Description |
|---|---|
| **Single Column** | All widgets in one vertical stack |
| **Two Columns** | A full-width area on top, with two equal columns below |
| **Three Columns** | A full-width area on top, with three equal columns below |
| **Sidebar Right** | A full-width area on top, with a wide main area and a narrow sidebar |

To change the layout:

1. Enter **edit mode** by clicking the **Reorder** button.
2. Click the **Layout** button in the toolbar.
3. Select your preferred layout from the picker dialog.
4. Your widgets will be automatically redistributed into the new zones.
5. Click **Done** to save.

## Resetting to Defaults

To restore the dashboard to its original configuration:

1. Enter edit mode by clicking **Reorder**.
2. Click the **Reset** button in the toolbar.

This removes all customizations and returns to the default widget arrangement and layout.

## Plugin Widgets

If you have plugins installed, they may register additional widgets that appear automatically on your dashboard. These widgets integrate seamlessly with the customization system — you can toggle, reorder, and move them just like built-in widgets.
