# Account Management

## Deleting Your Account

We respect your right to be forgotten. You can fully delete your account and all associated data from Ari at any time without administrative intervention.

### What is deleted?
*   **User Account**: Your login credentials and profile.
*   **Contacts**: All your address book records.
*   **Groups**: Your contact groups.
*   **Audit Logs**: History of changes related to your account.
*   **Settings**: All preferences and configurations.
*   **API Tokens**: All active sessions and refresh tokens.

### How to delete
1.  Navigate to **Settings**.
2.  Select **Delete Account** from the sidebar.
3.  Read the confirmation modal carefully.
4.  Click **Delete Account**.

**There is no undo.** Once deleted, your data is scrubbed from the database via cascade deletion protocols.
